Custom Tailoring

From first vision to final stitch — our five-step artisan process ensures your wedding attire is a true reflection of who you are.

Step One

Choose Your Design

Browse our signature lookbook and sample collection. Our consultants understand your event, your personality, and the aesthetic you're aiming for — then guide you to the designs that will make you feel extraordinary.

I
II

Step Two

Select Your Fabric

Choose from our curated library of imported and local fabrics — Banarasi silk brocades, Italian velvet, premium organza, and more. We'll help you match fabric weight and colour to your occasion and season.

Step Three

Precision Measurements

Our master tailor takes over 22 measurements to map your body perfectly. For overseas clients, we provide a detailed self-measurement guide with video support — ensuring a precise fit from afar.

IV

Step Four

Fitting Sessions

We schedule one to three fittings depending on complexity. Each session is an opportunity to refine the silhouette, adjust embroidery placement, and perfect every detail until it is immaculate.

Step Five

Final Delivery

Your completed ensemble is steam-pressed, inspected, and presented in our signature packaging. Delivery to your door, or collected in our boutique — your wedding wardrobe, complete and perfect.

Who We Are

Born in Sialkot,
Trusted Across Pakistan

BinDawood Dulha House began as a single family workshop tucked in the heart of Dara Arraian, Sialkot — a city long celebrated for its craft and precision. What started as a neighbourhood tailoring house became, over generations, one of the region's most respected names in luxury groom wear.

We believe that a wedding is not merely an occasion — it is a chapter in a family's story. The groom's attire carries the weight of that moment. It must be worthy of the memory. That conviction drives every decision we make, from the thread we source to the final press before delivery.

Today BinDawood dresses grooms from Sialkot to Birmingham, from Lahore to Toronto. Our collection spans ivory-embroidered sherwanis, jewelled prince coats, brocade jodhpuris, and everything the complete dulha needs — all crafted in-house by master karigars who have devoted their lives to this art.

Help Centre

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit or order. Still have a question? WhatsApp us anytime.

Do you offer custom stitching and tailoring? +

Yes — custom tailoring is at the heart of what we do. Every garment can be made to your exact measurements, preferred fabric, and design specifications. Our master tailors have decades of experience crafting bespoke groom wear for all occasions.

How long does tailoring take? +

Standard custom orders typically take 2–4 weeks depending on complexity and embroidery requirements. For simpler designs, we can often complete in 1–2 weeks. We also offer an express service for urgent orders — please WhatsApp us to discuss your timeline.

Can Overseas Pakistanis place orders? +

Absolutely. We regularly serve clients in the UK, USA, Canada, UAE, Saudi Arabia, and beyond. We provide a detailed measurement guide, offer WhatsApp or video call consultations, and ship internationally via courier. Your order arrives safely packaged and ready to wear.

How many fitting sessions are included? +

We include up to three fitting sessions for custom orders. The first fitting checks the basic silhouette, the second refines the fit and embroidery placement, and the final fitting confirms everything is perfect. We will not let you leave unless the outfit is flawless.

What fabrics do you use? +

We work with an extensive range including Italian and Banarasi silk brocades, premium velvet, organza, georgette, cotton-silk blends, and imported suiting fabrics. Our fabric library is updated seasonally. During your consultation you can touch and choose from the full range.

Do you accept urgent or last-minute orders? +

Yes — we have a dedicated express tailoring team for urgent orders. Depending on the garment complexity, we can complete some pieces in as little as 3–5 days. Please contact us immediately via WhatsApp with your wedding date so we can assess feasibility.

What occasions do your groom collections cover? +

We dress grooms for every wedding ceremony: Barat (the main event), Walima, Mehndi, and Nikah. We also create formal attire for family events and special occasions. Our consultants will guide you to the right look for each function.

How do I book an appointment? +

You can book via our online appointment form on this website, WhatsApp us directly at +92-333-777-7882, or simply walk in during business hours (Monday–Saturday, 10 AM – 8 PM). We recommend booking 3–6 months before your wedding for custom orders.

Do you provide groom turbans (kula) and accessories? +

Yes — we have an extensive range of traditional groom turbans in maroon, ivory, black, champagne, and more. Our consultants will help you match the kula perfectly to your attire. We also offer complete styling guidance for the full groom look.
